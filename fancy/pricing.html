Pricing

Honest pricing, personally quoted.

Private tours priced per person, quoted per trip. Here is how it works and what is always included.

Our tours are private by default, priced per person, and shaped to the group and the day. Rather than publish a dense matrix of numbers, we quote each trip so the price reflects what it actually is. Not what a generic booking engine guesses it should be.

Here's the honest version of how pricing works, what's included, and roughly what to expect.

The Short Version

Three tour formats, personalized pricing

We run Half-Day Tours (roughly 4-5 hours), Full-Day Tours (roughly 8-10 hours), and Bespoke Expeditions (multi-day, fully custom, including the Mighty 5 and wider Grand Circle).

Each format has a base per-person rate. That rate goes down as group size grows, because our costs don't scale one-for-one with headcount. A group of six pays less per person than a group of two.

Peak-season dates (March-May, September-October) run slightly higher than shoulder and off-season. Special one-off dates. Sunrise-to-sunset Arches, photography-focused itineraries, multi-park combos. Are quoted case by case.

What's Always Included

The price is the price

Door-to-door transport

We pick you up and drop you off at your lodging in the Moab area. No meeting points, no parking lots.

Park entry & permit fees

Arches, Canyonlands, Dead Horse Point, BLM. All covered. You don't pay anything at the gate.

Snacks & cold drinks

Water, sparkling water, sodas, real snacks. Picnic lunch included on every full-day tour.

Digital photo album

Your guide captures the day. You get a shareable gallery after the tour. No phone juggling, no bad selfies.

Local expert guide

Our guides live in Moab year-round and carry current Wilderness First Aid certifications.

All the gear

Optical binoculars, trekking poles if you want them, first aid, shade canopy. Nothing to buy or bring beyond your own clothes.

What Affects Your Quote

Why we don't publish a price list

Group size, season, tour length, pickup location, whether the day is a straight park tour or a bespoke build with specific destinations. These all move the number. Two couples booking the same Arches half-day at the same rate doesn't make sense if one is doing 9am-1pm and the other is doing sunrise-to-late-morning with a photography focus.

Rather than force everyone into the same box so we can post a tidy table, we quote each tour personally. Send dates, group size, and roughly what's catching your eye, and we come back with a quote and a plan. The quote is flat. What you're quoted is what you pay. No checkout-time fees, no fuel surcharges, no tip line assumed into the total.

If you want a rough order-of-magnitude before you reach out, ask. We'll give you a range on the spot.

Bespoke & Multi-Day

The full-expedition tier

Our Bespoke Expeditions are the most flexible and most personal tier. Multi-day itineraries. Sometimes a two-day Arches and Canyonlands combo, sometimes a five-day Mighty 5 loop through Zion, Bryce, Capitol Reef, Arches, and Canyonlands, sometimes a Grand Circle expedition that adds the Grand Canyon and northern Arizona.

Bespoke pricing is quoted per trip, not per day. These are the bookings we put the most planning into. Logistics, lodging coordination, meals, permits, guides, photography. So the only thing left for the guest is to show up. Highest-ticket bookings, deepest planning.

If that's the kind of trip in front of you, see the Bespoke page for the full scope, or send a note about what you're dreaming up and we'll come back with a proposal.

Booking & Payment

How it works

Once your quote is confirmed, a deposit holds the date. The balance is due a few days before the tour. We accept cards, ACH, and wire for larger bookings. You'll receive a clear invoice and confirmation, not a vague email with a link.

Cancellation terms are on the invoice and are among the friendlier ones in the industry. We know trips get rearranged. Weather days are never penalized; we reschedule or refund in full.
