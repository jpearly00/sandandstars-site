Nighthike

A private evening moonlight walk (about 3 hours) near Moab. On full-moon weeks the desert lights itself; on dark-sky nights the Milky Way is the main event.

Tour at a glance

Duration optionsHalf day (4 hr)
GroupPrivate to your group

About this tour

On full-moon weeks, the desert lights itself. Walk a familiar trail by moonlight, cooler, quieter, and transformed beyond recognition.

On dark-sky nights, the Milky Way is the main event. About three hours in the evening, timed to the moon and the season.

Highlights

  • Moonlit desert trail
  • Dark-sky stargazing
  • Cooler evening temperatures
  • A familiar landscape transformed

  • All logistics and route planning

Frequently asked questions

When can we do a nighthike?
It's timed to the moon and season, full-moon weeks for moonlight walks, dark-sky nights for stargazing. Tell us your dates and we'll find the best window.
How long is it?
About three hours in the evening, at an easy pace on a familiar trail.
Do we need special gear?
Just comfortable shoes and a layer for the cooler evening. Your guide brings the rest.

Every Sand & Stars day starts with a short conversation. Tell us your dates and group size; we usually reply same day.
