Best of Moab to Dead Horse Point

A private half-day tour that packs ancient rock art, dinosaur tracks, a short arch hike, remote 4x4 roads, and the famous Dead Horse Point overlook into one varied day, more than most full-day tours elsewhere.

About this tour

A half day that packs in more variety than most full-day tours elsewhere: ancient rock art, dinosaur tracks, a short arch hike, remote 4x4 dirt roads, secluded overlooks, and Dead Horse Point itself, the view that convinced Hollywood.

Stand above a perfect oxbow in the Colorado River, then keep walking for solitude most visitors never find. Choose six hours for more time at each stop and a slower pace through the canyon rim country.

Frequently asked questions

What makes this different from the Canyonlands tour?
This half day samples the widest variety near Moab, rock art, dino tracks, 4x4, and Dead Horse Point, while the Canyonlands tour focuses on the big mesa-top vistas of Island in the Sky.
Is this good for families?
Yes. The walking is light and optional, and the mix of dinosaurs, rock art, and 4x4 driving keeps all ages engaged.
Is Dead Horse Point open year-round?
Yes, this tour runs year-round. Winter brings quiet trails and crisp, clear overlooks.
